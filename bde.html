Beginner Driver Education Program

Ministry of Transportation Approved Basic Driving Education Course

About The Instructor

This course is taught by an instructor over 18 years with a long teaching experience from various reputable driving school in Mississauga and Brampton.


This course is designed for students who have little or no driving experience and need to complete the Beginners Driver Education (BDE) course. BDE course is recognized by the insurance industry as proof of course completion. BDE Graduates may be eligible for an insurance discount as it depends on the insurance company BDE Graduates are eligible to book their G1 exit road test in 8 months instead of 12 months. Please note that in order to get a certificate you need to complete the whole course within six months from the day you register*

Package Includes

This program has 3 components.
1- Twenty (20) Hours of Classroom training
2- Ten (10) Hours of Drive Online home assignment |
3- Ten (10) Hours of In-Car training

Upon successful completion it may save you on auto insurance and allows you to go for G2 license in 8 months time.

*Note that the MTO requirement can change without notice

Benefits

1. Save 4 months on your G1 road test waiting period
2. Flexible Course allows you to start any time you want
3. We offer pick up and drop off at your home for in car lessons
